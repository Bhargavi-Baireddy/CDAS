OVERVIEW

What is CDAS?

CDAS (Centralized Delay Analysis System) is a purpose-built operations platform for integrated steel plants — designed to capture, analyse, and reduce equipment and production delays across all departments.

🏭
Plant-Wide Coverage
Covers every shop — Blast Furnace, Steel Melting, Rolling Mills, Coke Ovens, Sinter Plant, and more — in a single unified system.
⚡
Real-Time Entry
Field operators log delay events the moment they occur. Timestamps, durations, and root-cause agency are captured automatically.
🔐
Secure Role Access
Operators, supervisors, managers, and system administrators each see only what they need — enforced by server-side role controls.
How It Works
1
Log Delay
Operator selects shop, equipment & agency; enters time and description.
2
Auto-Calculate
System computes duration, timestamps entry, and assigns a unique ID.
3
Review & Approve
Supervisors review, close, or escalate incidents via the listing view.
4
Analyse & Export
Managers generate reports, trend charts, and export CSV/PDF for review.

System Modules

Six integrated modules cover every aspect of industrial delay management — from first entry to executive reporting.

Dashboard
Live KPI cards showing total delays, active incidents, downtime hours, and trend charts across all shops for the current shift or day.

Delay Entry
Structured form to log delays: shop code, equipment, sub-equipment, responsible agency (Mechanical / Electrical / Operations / Shutdown), time range, and description.
OPERATORS

Delay Listing
Searchable, filterable log of all delays with status tracking (Active / Closed), pagination, Excel and PDF export.
SUPERVISORS

Reports
Configurable reports filtered by shop, date range, agency, and equipment. Trend line chart + stacked bar breakdown with summary footer.

Analytics
